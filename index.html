What we handle

Every Esevai service, under one roof

Visit us or upload your documents online — we handle the paperwork so you don't have to chase government counters.

📜

Community Certificate

Caste and community certificates for education, government schemes and reservation benefits.

🏡

Nativity Certificate

Proof of origin for jobs, admissions and government applications requiring place-of-birth verification.

💰

Income Certificate

Income certificates for scholarships, bank loans, fee concessions and government welfare schemes.

🏅

OBC Certificate

Other Backward Class certificates for central and state government reservations and schemes.

🪪

New PAN Card

Fresh PAN card applications for individuals — processed quickly with minimal documents needed.

✏️

PAN Card Correction

Name, date of birth and address corrections on existing PAN cards with proper documentation.

🗳️

New Voter ID

New voter registration for first-time voters — only Aadhaar and a photo required.

🔄

Voter ID Correction

Name, address, photo and date-of-birth corrections on existing Voter ID / EPIC cards.

🛒

New Ration Card

New family ration card applications including all family member Aadhaar linking and gas bill linking.
